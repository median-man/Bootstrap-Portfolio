About Me

I'm an enthusiastic and passionate web developer keen to create useful and interesting applications and websites on a team of like-minded developers.

Currently, I am a full-time student in the Coding Bootcamp at UC San Diego Extension. Prior to joining the bootcamp, I spent ten years working in retail at REI as a member of a diverse team of individuals. Together, we were able to achieve record setting performances for the business unit in between trips together to ski, hike, camp, climb, and otherwise explore the outdoors. My rolls during this time included management, office administration, sales, and customer service. Prior to working at REI, I attended Chapman University where I earned a B.S. in Business Administration.
